## 😊 Expression Library

### 1. Happy (Default)
- Eyes: fully open, bright, catchlights prominent
- Mouth: wide smile, slight "U" shape
- Eyebrows: relaxed, slightly raised
- Body: standing straight, slight lean forward

### 2. Curious
- Eyes: wide open, one slightly larger
- Mouth: small "o" shape
- Eyebrows: raised unevenly (one higher)
- Body: leaning forward, head tilted

### 3. Surprised
- Eyes: maximum wide, circular
- Mouth: open "O"
- Eyebrows: raised high
- Body: slight lean backward, hands up

### 4. Thinking
- Eyes: looking up-left, half-lidded
- Mouth: slight pucker to one side
- Eyebrows: one raised, one lowered
- Body: hand on chin, weight on one foot

### 5. Celebrating
- Eyes: closed crescents (happy squint)
- Mouth: wide open smile, teeth visible
- Eyebrows: raised high
- Body: jumping, arms up, confetti implied

### 6. Sleepy
- Eyes: half-closed, droopy
- Mouth: yawning or small smile
- Eyebrows: relaxed, low
- Body: slight slouch, rubbing eye

## 🎬 Animation Principles
- **Squash and stretch:** Apply to all jumps, bounces, and emotional reactions
- **Anticipation:** 2-3 frame wind-up before major movements
- **Overshoot:** Slight overshoot on all transitions (elastic feel)
- **Secondary motion:** Hair tufts follow head movement with 2-frame delay
- **Blink rate:** Every 3-5 seconds, quick double-blink
- **Idle animation:** Gentle sway, occasional look around, blink cycle
